Markets for Traders

This page documents the Market Mechanics & Execution pillar of the Governance-First algorithmic trading curriculum. It is explicitly not about alpha discovery. Its objective is to train practitioners to design trading systems that remain coherent under execution costs, liquidity constraints, regime shifts, and funding stress.

The Two-Book Sequence

Book I — The ABC of Trading

Mechanism-first market literacy. Liquidity, carry, correlation, funding, and regimes as first-class objects.

Book II — Markets for Traders

Ten execution laboratories that force costs, constraints, and failure under stress.
